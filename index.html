Research Interest

I am primarily interested in two topics. My focus is on building a storage engine for cloud-native databases. Currently, I am working towards optimizing existing storage engines like RocksDB to better suit cloud environment. However, I believe more needs to be done, and I feel excited to rethink the original storage engine design and come up with an better one suitable for cloud-native databases. Besides, I'm also interested in optimizing storage for AI/ML training. I believe there's considerable potential to improve storage requirements for AI training, whether on a single machine or in a distributed setting, and I am eager to explore this area further.
